Privacy Policy for Amazon Summarize Chrome Extension

Last updated: July 24, 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Amazon Summarize Chrome Extension ("we", "our", or "the Extension") collects, uses, and handles your information. Our Extension uses Perplexity AI locally to summarize Amazon product pages. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure experience while using our Extension.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide to Us

The Amazon Summarize Extension does not require you to create an account or directly provide any personal information.

Information We Access and Process

Our Extension accesses and processes the following information:

  • Content of Amazon product pages you visit while the Extension is active
  • Your interaction with the Extension, such as when you request a summary

It's important to note that all processing of Amazon product page content is done locally on your device using the Perplexity AI model. We do not collect, transmit, or store this information on any external servers.

How We Use Your Information

The information accessed by our Extension is used solely for the purpose of generating summaries of Amazon product pages. Specifically:

  1. To create concise summaries of product descriptions, features, and reviews
  2. To improve your shopping experience by providing quick, AI-generated overviews of products

Data Storage and Transmission

  • All data processing occurs locally on your device.
  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information or browsing data to external servers.
  • The Perplexity AI model runs entirely on your local machine, ensuring that your data never leaves your device.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

As we do not collect or store any user data, we do not share or disclose any personal information to third parties.

Data Security

Since all operations are performed locally on your device, the security of your data primarily depends on your own device's security measures. We recommend keeping your browser and extensions up to date and using strong security practices on your device.

Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over the Extension's access to your data:

  • You can disable or remove the Extension at any time through your Chrome browser settings.
  • You can choose when to use the Extension to generate summaries.

Children's Privacy

Our Extension is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on the Chrome Web Store and updating the "Last updated" date.
